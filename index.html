Our Services

Our services cover from domestic to abroad in pet care section anywhere 24/7

We are a group of professional pet experts coming from different fields in animal care sectors. We are happy to be trusted to take care of your loved pets with all dedication.

Outdoor activities

We ensure that your pet enjoys walking and outdoor activities with us. There are more activities than just regular walking, for example: jogging in the forest, or visiting animal parks where your pets are allowed to roam free — are becoming more common in many cities, or together playing Frisbee.

Namnam and Chill

We have a partnership with many local pet food brands in the town that can give your pet the best taste with a full of nutrition portion for the growth and development of your pet. So apart from a long walk and a good meal, check out the sofa and get to snoozing. .

Be here with you anytime you need

We offer a second home for your pet when you are away for a sunshine trip on the beach of Magaluf, or surfing in Pattaya or wherever your destination is. We are also here to help you planing your first trip with your pet in any corner of the world.

Workout together

Special care aims to help you resolve pet issues related to pet behaviors, but also offering other training for your pets. We offer house training and agility trail training for your pet.
